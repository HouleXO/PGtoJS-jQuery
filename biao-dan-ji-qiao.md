**jquery如何设置与去除disabled属性？五种方法**

//两种方法设置disabled属性

```js
 $('#areaSelect').attr("disabled",true);
 $('#areaSelect').attr("disabled","disabled");
```

//三种方法移除disabled属性

```js
 $('#areaSelect').attr("disabled",false);
 $('#areaSelect').removeAttr("disabled");
 $('#areaSelect').attr("disabled","");
```
